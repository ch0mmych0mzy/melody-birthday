What I Want For Us Going Forward

As we grow deeper in this friendship, here are some things I would love for us to build together:

Deeper Communication

No more small talk or abbreviations. Use full punctuation and proper text forms in our messages. I want us to communicate with depth and intention, taking the time to express ourselves properly. Call me when you miss me. Text me when you think of me or I cross your mind without hesitating. Live more and Love more.

Complete Honesty

When I ask how you are, I want the full details - not just "I am fine" or "I am sad." Tell me why you feel that way, what happened, and all the details. Let us share everything without holding back.

Being Each Other's Go-To

I want us to be each other's first call when something happens - good or bad. No more bottling things up or worrying alone. We should help each other through everything life brings.

Matching Words With Actions

What you say should align with what you do. No inconsistencies between your words and your behavior. I promise to do the same for you.

Remember The Details

No forgetfulness about important conversations, dates, or events. Even the tiny details matter when they are about each other. Our memories of each other show how much we truly care.

Absolute Confidentiality

What we share stays between us. Be trustworthy, faithful, loyal, and learn from each other the positives. No snitching or sharing our business with others. Our private matters remain private - no exceptions.

Growth Together

I want to help you become who you are meant to be and actualize your dreams. We should teach each other with love, correct each other with kindness, and grow together.

Open Confidence

Be more confident with me. Open up fully without holding back. Let us create a space where we can both be completely ourselves without fear or hesitation.

Mutual Reciprocity

What I give, you return. What you give, I return. This balance keeps our friendship healthy and strong.
